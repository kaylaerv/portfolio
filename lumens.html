Lumens Website and Email Design & Development

Lumens was in the process of refreshing their brand and needed a redesigned email template library. Their emails weren’t responsive and left users with an incoherent mobile viewing experience.

Responsibilities

  • UI/UX Design
  • Front-End Development

Tools used

  • Sketch
  • HTML
  • CSS

The brand refresh had to be completed on a very tight deadline, so a lot of the work had to be done all at once. While the logo was being redesigned I worked to create the new look and feel for web materials through the email template designs

I began by creating some basic wireframes for one of our previous emails. It included all the elements from before, but allowed me to focus on how they would appear on screen sizes less than 600px wide.

The wireframes showcased the flexibility of the template’s interchangeable parts. For example, a section of individual products could be swapped with a section of designers and their accompanying biographies.

The new design portrays the love for modern design that Lumens employees and customers share. The design was encompassed by plentiful white space broken up by product imagery and minimal typography.

Before the rebrand, Lumens' website, emails, and print materials were inconsistent in look and feel; Now, they all work together. The design system I had a large part in creating ended up being the basis of all digital design for the new Lumens.
