Auction Marketplace App

Pickles Asia needed a mobile application that would allow them to reach an audience of Malaysian car dealerships and car buyers through vehicle auctions. The app was crucial for the market because insurance companies needed to be able to quickly make a profit from salvage vehicles because they are commonly stolen in Malaysia.

Responsibilities

  • UI/UX Design

Tools used

  • Adobe XD

The Process

First we looked into competitors (MUV, carlist.my). We gathered our research on the differences and requirements and began constructing the information architecture.

Next, I created wireframes for each of the pages. Through our research we discovered that things are a lot different in the vehicle industry in Malaysia than they are in the US. Malaysian car dealers are searching for vehicles differently than people in the US are. They want to find the cheapest deals as fast as possible and they would rather see a sortable list of vehicles than an advanced search with filters.

We had a lot of interesting surprises arise when working on the wireframes, like the fact that most of our userbase aren't familiar with email and do not even have email addresses. This, along with the convenience of easy viewing access led us to the decision to create a guest log in experience where users are able to view important parts of the app before deciding to sign up.

The wireframes went through many rounds of revisions and edits before we moved on to high fidelity mockups.

Finally I added color and extra design elements, while sticking to the framework 7 vue.js components which the project was being developed in.
